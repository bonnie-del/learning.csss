RULES AND REGULATIONS

Our rules and regulations are detailed below

Academic Life

To achieve the highest academic standards, as is the aspiration of every one of us, every student is required to develop and maintain a culture conducive for academic excellence.
The following regulations therefore need to be strictly observed.

  1. Students shall attend all lessons and preps except where they are exempted or in the case of illness.
  2. Every student is expected to maintain silence in the classroom and around the college premises.
  3. Dismissal /Expulsion, discontinuation of student at Baraton College will be recorded on the student’s permanent record with the reasons given.
  4. Suspension and Termination of student for a defined period of time, conditions for return to be stated.

Below are some offences which if committed by a student, shall call for an instant ONE YEAR suspension pending investigation. These offenses include those that deliberately violate the code of conduct such as:-

  • Personal and physical assault on a staff, faculty or student.
  • Possession or use a dangerous weapon.
  • Drunkenness or being under the influences of illegal drugs or tobacco
